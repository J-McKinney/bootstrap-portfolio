About-Me

Well, what's to say? I'm 33 years old, I just got married last September, life is good. Just repetitive... My life outside of work is great, spontaneous, and exciting. That's why I decided to go for a career change. I've seen it all and done it all, as a bartender. I started in the food and beverage industry when I was 16 years old. I started as a dishwasher, busboy, server, etc... And, so on. And, so forth. After 17 years of doing this and the wide experience of fine dining and "THE RUSH" of casual dining. Nothing is unexpected anymore. So, I got bored and picked up hobbies, to unleash a more creative side of myself. While still working full-time, I decided to do a little wood working. It started off small, then it got bigger. Along with building my own workbench to the patio furniture on my deck. What can I say? The feeling you get when a plan goes into action and the end result is even better than you thought it would be. Unbelievable!!! You don't want to stop. You want to go out and build a house, from the ground up. The only, tiniest, minute, pocket-sized problem with that is.... After, I got done with those projects, I had nothing in my pockets to afford new material, for the new projects. So, looking for an alternative. I had a friend suggest the tech frontier. So, I looked into it. We live in the digital age, the age of computers and technology. You want to know what I saw? I saw a "free, limitless, canvas of creativivty". HTML is my hammer and saw, CSS is my endless lumber yard. Now, that I have started this. I don't want it to end...
